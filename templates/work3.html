Which Animal am I? Performance by Different ML Models


Welcome to the Animal Classifier! Check this Machine Learning tool out by uploading an image of an animal and test three powerful algorithms: MobileNetV2, Simple CNN, and ResNet-50. While the Simple CNN and ResNet-50 were trained on a diverse dataset of 151 animals, MobileNetV2 uses a pre-trained model (mobilnet_v2-b0353104.pth) known for its efficiency. The models have not yet been been optimalized by fine-tuning the super-parameters. Some rare animals might not be known to the CNN and ResNet-50 models, so surprises are always around the corner!

Got a photo of an animal? Upload it and see which model nails the classification. Discover how modern tech recognizes the animal kingdom.

Simple CNN and ResNet50 Models not yet available due to large size, will be added soon!
