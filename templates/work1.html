Scouting Football Talents with FIFA Data


the fast-paced world of football, spotting emerging talents is a game-changer. In this project talents and upcoming football players under-21 are analysed to see if they would be a worthwhile investment.

Using FIFA player data encompassing valuation, release clause, age, current and potential capabilities, this project dives into a player's potential in comparison to the valuation and release clause.
